Privacy Policy

Welcome Bot · Last updated: July 20, 2026

Welcome Bot ("the Bot") is a Discord bot. This page explains what data it collects, why, and what happens to it. We keep this short because we collect very little.

What we collect

  • Discord User ID. When you use game commands (like !fight), we store a record keyed to your numeric Discord user ID, containing your in-bot game state: captured characters, levels, inventory, and fight history. We do not store your username, avatar, email, or any other Discord profile data.
  • Message text you send to voice commands. If you use a voice-generation command (e.g. !leosays), the text you provide is sent to a third-party text-to-speech service (FakeYou) solely to generate the audio clip you requested. We don't store that text ourselves.

We do not read, log, or store the content of your other messages. We do not collect IP addresses, device information, or analytics.

Why we collect it

Solely to make the Bot's features work: your Discord ID is the key that lets the game remember your characters between sessions, and voice-command text is forwarded only to generate the audio you asked for.

Sharing and selling

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone. The only exception is the third-party service noted above (FakeYou), which only ever receives the exact text you submit to a voice command — nothing else.

Storage and retention

Game data is stored in the Bot's own database for as long as your Discord ID is active in it. You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Contact below), and it will be removed.

Children's privacy

The Bot is intended for use in accordance with Discord's own Terms of Service, which require users to be at least 13 years old (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from anyone below that age beyond what Discord itself already requires to operate an account.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change. Continued use of the Bot after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions, or want your data deleted? Reach out via Discord.
